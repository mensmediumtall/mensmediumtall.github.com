Fat 2013-02-10

Negative Feedback

A little while ago we released this comic in which we used the word fat and we had one complaint that it was offensive.

The Defense Takes the Stand

I don't disagree that a person would feel offended. People are offended by lots of things.

However, I want to put forth a case as to why it was appropriate (and perhaps necessary) to use the word fat in all of its glory.

(read through to the end before getting in a huff, there's some legitimate reasoning here)

America has a lot of overweight people (overweight is so much longer of a word than fat). Although some people claim that obesity (a word which implies not just an image issue, but also a medical condition) is heriditary and yada yada. I'll argue FALSE. False in the sense that weight gain or loss is more strongly associated with calorie intake than any other factor.
